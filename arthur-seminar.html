P-adic Arthur Packets Seminar - Spring 2024

In Adams, Barbasch and Vogan's book on the local Langlands correspondence for real groups, the authors introduced a geometric construction of local Arthur packets using perverse sheaves and microlocal analysis. A similar geometrization was recently formulated by Cunningham et. al. for p-adic groups providing a unified framework to work with Arthur packets.

In this reading seminar we will work through the two papers "Arthur packets for p-adic groups by way of microlocal vanishing cycles of perverse sheaves with examples" (2017) and "Arthur packets for G2 and perverse sheaves on cubics" (2020).
