Postdoctoral Position in Representation Theory

I am recruiting one postdoctoral researcher for a two-year position, at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. The expected start date is September 2026, though this is flexible.

The position is in representation theory, broadly construed. Topics of particular interest include p-adic representation theory, harmonic analysis, Langlands program, Hecke algebras, periodically graded Lie algebras, and geometric representation theory.

Applicants should hold a PhD in mathematics (or expect to complete one before the start date) in a relevant area.

To apply, please email me your CV and a research statement. Deadline: 30 June.
