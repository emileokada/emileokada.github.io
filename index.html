Research

My research focuses on local aspects of the Langlands programme. In particular, I study character singularities of p-adic groups and their connections to the geometry of automorphic forms, Galois representations, and the Aubert-Zelevinsky duality. I am also interested in the role of maximal compact subgroups in representation theory.
