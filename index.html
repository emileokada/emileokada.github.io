Emile Okada

Peng Tsu Ann Assistant Professor, National University of Singapore

Email: lastname at nus.edu.sg

I am a mathematician at the National University of Singapore working on representation theory, with a focus on p-adic groups, character theory, Hecke algebras, and the Langlands program.
